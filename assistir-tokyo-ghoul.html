東京グール (Tokyo Ghoul)

2013

♱

Manga

Ação

Synopsis: Think of a story in which you are the protagonist. It would probably be a tragedy, wouldn't it? Well, that's Kaneki's story... Strange murders are happening in Tokyo. Due to liquid evidence at the scene, police concluded that the attacks were the result of of a "eater" of a ghoul type. Kaneki is an 18-year-old young man attending college, passionate about Japanese novels, he and his friend Hide, create the theory that ghouls are imitating humans, which is why they have never been seen. Bad they know this theory could be true.
